# Mental Health Trends and Associated Factors Among Working Populations

## Project Overview

This project analyzes mental health trends and associated factors among working populations using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into stress levels, coping struggles, treatment-seeking behavior, occupational influences, and other factors affecting mental wellbeing.

The objective is to transform mental health data into meaningful visual insights that support understanding and decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives

- Assess treatment-seeking behaviour among respondents.
- Examine stress levels and coping struggles.
- Analyze occupational and behavioural factors affecting mental health.
- Identify factors influencing mental health outcomes.
- Evaluate the impact of indoor stay on stress and coping struggles.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft PowerPoint

## Dataset Features

The dataset contains information related to:

- Gender
- Occupation
- Treatment
- Family History
- Growing Stress
- Mood Swings
- Coping Struggles
- Social Weakness
- Work Interest
- Days Indoors
- Country

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview Dashboard
Provides a summary of key mental health indicators using KPI cards and distribution charts.

### 2. Stress and Behaviour Analysis
Explores stress levels, coping struggles, mood swings, and behavioural patterns.

### 3. Occupational and Social Analysis
Examines relationships between occupation, social factors, and mental health outcomes.

### 4. Key Influencers Analysis
Identifies the major factors influencing stress, coping struggles, and treatment behaviour.

---

## Key Insights

- Stress levels varied across occupations and behavioural groups.
- Longer indoor stay was associated with higher coping struggles.
- Mood swings were linked with social weakness.
- Work interest influenced growing stress levels.
- Mental health outcomes were affected by behavioural, occupational, and environmental factors.
